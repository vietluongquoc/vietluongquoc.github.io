Aircraft's Landing Gear

Aircraft landing gear equipped with a magnetorheological (MR) damper is a semi-active system that contains nonlinear behavior, disturbances, uncertainties, and delay times.

INFORMATION

PROFILE

Quoc Viet, Luong was born in Kon Tum City, Viet Nam. He obtained a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from the Ho Chi Minh University of Technology in 2012, and a Master of Engineering from the same institution in 2015. In 2022, He then pursued a Ph.D. degree from the School of Mechanical Engineering, Korea Aerospace University, South Korea. Where he focused on improving the performance of intelligent landing gear equipped with a Magnetorheological damper. Currently, he is interested in developing an artificial-intelligent system, web-of-thing system, robotics, and smart material.
